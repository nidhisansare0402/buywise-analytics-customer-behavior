# BuyWise Analytics: Customer Behavior & Retail Strategy

## Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior to uncover key insights that help improve revenue, customer engagement, and marketing strategies.

The analysis is performed using Python, PostgreSQL, and Power BI.

---

## Objectives

* Identify high-value customers
* Analyze impact of discounts and subscriptions
* Understand revenue drivers across categories and demographics
* Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## Tech Stack

* Python (Pandas, Data Cleaning, Feature Engineering)
* PostgreSQL (Advanced SQL, Window Functions)
* Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)

---

## Key Features

* Customer segmentation (New, Regular, Loyal)
* High-value customer identification
* Revenue contribution analysis
* Discount and subscription impact analysis
* Interactive Power BI dashboard

## Key Insights

* Loyal customers contribute the highest revenue
* Discounts do not significantly increase spending
* Clothing category contributes ~45% of total revenue
* Subscription model is not driving higher revenue

---

## Business Recommendations

* Convert regular customers into loyal customers through targeted engagement strategies
* Reduce dependency on discounts and focus on profitability-driven promotions
* Strengthen high-performing categories like Clothing while improving weaker segments
* Implement product bundling strategies to increase average purchase value
* Redesign subscription programs with added customer benefits
* Target younger demographics with personalized marketing campaigns
* Introduce VIP programs for high-value customers

---

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data-driven insights can help businesses improve decision-making and optimize strategies.
